Project choice

Team Monkague has chosen to do James's idea of a Character Info database for "StarWars the Clone Wars" animated series. This may be potentally expanded to other cannon projects linked to this show (e.g. "The Bad Batch"). This will function similar to websites like IMDB, but focoused around the shows characters, affiliations, episodes and factions. This also maybe expanded to add infomation about directors, show runners or voice actors.

Progress

So far the team has organised into working on this page and the proposal page. We have made a prototype to test some ideas, made a database diagram and mapped out the routes.
