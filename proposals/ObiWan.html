OBI-WAN-KENOBI

Obi-Wan Kenobi, also known as Ben Kenobi, is a legendary Jedi Master in the Star Wars universe. He is known for his wisdom, bravery, and mentorship of Luke Skywalker. Obi-Wan Kenobi's character is portrayed by Alec Guinness in the original trilogy and by Ewan McGregor in the prequel trilogy.
