Online comic organiser

An online web app that allows users to search up the comics/books that they are currently reading/engaging with.

The reader will then be able to add the content onto their favourites list on their profile. then they will be able to track their progress, and share it with friends.

Users can will be able to comment on the content and share their thoughts on it, as well as see what other people think about it.

All of the data will be stored in a database including the users, and the contents.

You will be able to fill out a form to input various information, that will hopfully be sent back to the running web application server, and will be held within a database. The information to be sent to the database will include:

  • User name
  • Comic(s) in progress
  • Some sort of password
  • Chapters read of a series
  • Rating of a series
  • Thought and or comments

Features:

  • Track progress throughout a series
  • Comment on the content
  • Share content with friends
  • Search for content
  • Favourite content
  • View profiles
  • Filter through finished series and ongoing series
  • Track chapter count
  • Store and retrieve basic information including, author, title, release date, poublisher/studio
