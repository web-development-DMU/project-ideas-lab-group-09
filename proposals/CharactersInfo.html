Description

Star Wars: The Clone Wars is an animated series that explores the events of the Clone Wars, set between the prequel trilogy films "Attack of the Clones" and "Revenge of the Sith." The series follows Jedi Knights Anakin Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Ahsoka Tano as they lead the Galactic Republic's clone army against the Separatist Alliance, commanded by Count Dooku. The show delves into the characters and planets introduced in the prequel films, providing a deeper look into the untold stories of the Clone Wars era. It was created by George Lucas and produced by Lucasfilm Animation, with Dave Filoni serving as supervising director.
